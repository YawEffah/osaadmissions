OSA User Manual

Welcome to the OSA App User Manual! This guide will walk you through the features and functionalities
of the OSA Admissions platform, helping you navigate the admission process with ease.
#AdmissionsMadeEasyWithOSA



Get Started Download App

Using the OSA App



1

Verifying Placement Status

Start Here

  • Open the OSA app or visit the web version
  • Navigate to the "Admission Verification" section
  • Enter your BECE index number and click "Submit."
  • If placed, you will be prompted to fill out the admission processing form

2

Filling the Admission Form

Complete Your Information

  • After verification, go to the "Admission Processing" section
  • Complete the form with the required details
  • Submit the form and proceed to make the payment

3

Making Payments

Secure Your Admission

  • Go to the "Payments" section
  • Select the type of payment (e.g., admission fee)
  • Choose your payment method (mobile money, credit/debit card)
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment
  • A receipt will be generated, which you can save or print

4

Downloading Admission Letter and Prospectus

Get Your Documents

  • After payment, go to the "Downloads" section
  • You will see options to download your admission letter and prospectus
  • Click on the respective links to download or print these documents
  • Fill and submit original copy of the admission letter when reporting to school
